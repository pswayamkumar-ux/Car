Our Story

Driving Excellence Since 2009

Rideez Car Rental was founded with a simple mission: to provide exceptional car rental experiences that combine quality, convenience, and affordability. What started as a small fleet of 10 vehicles has grown into one of the region's most trusted car rental companies.

Over the past 15 years, we've served over 50,000 customers, maintained a fleet of 100+ premium vehicles, and built a team of dedicated professionals who share our commitment to customer satisfaction.
